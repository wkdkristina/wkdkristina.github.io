# Case Study: Agency & Stakeholder Management
## The Challenge
The company was over-paying an external agency for “low value” SEO tasks while internal technical debt continued to mount. Communication between the agency and the internal Dev team had completely broken down.

## The Zero Waste Solution
1. **Agency Output Audit:** Performed a performance-to-spend audit that revealed 60% of external agency fees were being allocated to low-impact "surface-level" metadata fixes with zero conversion correlation.
2. **Resource Reallocation Pivot:** Orchestrated a strategic shift, redirecting $12,500/month from redundant agency retainers toward high-yield technical infrastructure and custom BigQuery/SQL dashboards.
3. **The Translation Layer:** Integrated SEO requirements directly into Engineering Jira sprints and Editorial guardrails, eliminating the "SEO Silo" and fostering a culture of unified technical growth.

## Tools Used
* Jira / Asana (Engineering Integration)
* BigQuery & SQL (Revenue Attribution)
* Financial Modeling (Agency ROI Analysis)
* SOP Development (Cross-Functional Workflows)

## The Business Impact
* **Fiscal Optimization:** Eliminated a six-figure annual overhead in redundant agency fees while increasing technical site performance by 20%.
* **Operational Velocity:** Reduced editorial publication lag by 40% and achieved a 90% completion rate on technical SEO engineering tickets.
* **Executive Buy-in:** Successfully pivoted SEO from an opaque "cost center" to a transparent "revenue engine" by providing clear ROI correlation to Finance and Leadership.

## The Deep Dive
* **Identifying Value Leakage**
I audited the existing agency output and found that the high volume of generic blog posts being produced had a low rank potential and even lower conversion rate. By visualizing this "Low-Impact" allocation, I secured stakeholder approval to pivot.

* **The Unified Solution**
To solve friction between departments, I replaced vague "SEO tickets" with Jira-integrated Technical PRDs (Product Requirement Documents). This gave Engineering the exact specifications they needed to execute without back-and-forth.
* **Strategic Alignment Matrix**
I mapped the specific pain points of each stakeholder group (Engineering, Editorial, and Executive) and provided automated solutions that turned SEO from a "hurdle" into a "helper."
